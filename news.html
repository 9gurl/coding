Zumba Class Announcement

Date: July 13, 2024

By: Jordan Villacorta

We are excited to announce that starting July 13 2024 Saturday we will be hosting Zumba classes in our community! Zumba is a fun and energetic fitness program that combines Latin and international music with dance moves. It's a great way to stay active and meet your neighbors.

Location: RPS2 Clubhouse

Day and Time: Saturdays and Sunday 7:30 am - 9:00 am

These classes are open to all residents of Ridgepoint Phase 2 Subdivision. Whether you're a beginner or experienced in Zumba, everyone is welcome to join in the fun and get moving. Please bring comfortable clothing, water, and your enthusiasm! We look forward to seeing you there.

If you have any questions, feel free to contact Jordan Villacorta via fb: jeo mv
